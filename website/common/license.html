Usage Guidelines

Academic Use

When using our datasets for academic research:

  • Cite the dataset in your publications
  • Acknowledge any modifications made to the original dataset
  • Share your findings with the community

Commercial Use

For commercial applications:

  • Include attribution in your documentation
  • Maintain transparency about data sources
  • Consider contributing improvements back to the community

Third-Party Content

Please note that while our dataset compilation is CC BY 4.0 licensed, individual articles and content within the datasets may be subject to their original authors' copyright and terms of use.

Users are responsible for:

  • Respecting original content creators' rights
  • Complying with platform-specific terms of service
  • Obtaining additional permissions if required
